DCRUST SGPA Calculator

This Calculator will help you calculate,visualize the effect of change in marks in your SGPA.

At DCRUST, every semester you are awarded a SGPA. At the end of course average of all SGPA is calculated as CGPA.

SGPA/CGPA can be converted to equivalent percentage by multiplying 9.5 to the SGPA/CGPA respectively.


For Demo purpose only. Author makes no claim of accuracy. All calculations are performed on your device. Your marks are never sent to server.
